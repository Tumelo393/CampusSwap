How CampusSwap Keeps You Safe

The Process

A Marketplace You Can Trust

Every feature is designed around the real safety concerns students face when trading on WhatsApp or Facebook groups.

1

Institutional Email Verification

Only students with .ac.bw email addresses from BQA-accredited institutions can join. A secure verification code is sent to your email — no manual review, no delays.

2

Seller Ratings & Transaction History

Like inDrive or Yango, every seller has a visible star rating and number of completed sales. The community self-regulates bad actors quickly.

3

QR Code Escrow System

Funds are held until the buyer scans the seller's QR code at handover, confirming the item matches the listing. This eliminates "not as described" fraud.

4

Safe Zone Meetup Points

Transactions happen at designated points in campus libraries and high-traffic areas — always monitored, always public, always recorded on CCTV.
